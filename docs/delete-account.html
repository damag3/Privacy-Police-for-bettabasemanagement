How to request account deletion

If you want to delete your BettaBase Management account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to bettafishapk@gmail.com from the email address registered in your account.
  2. In the subject line, write: “Account Deletion Request – BettaBase Management”.
  3. In the email, include:
