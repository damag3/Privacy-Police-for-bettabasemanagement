Delete Account – BettaBase Management

How to request account deletion

If you want to delete your BettaBase Management account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to bettafishapk@gmail.com from the email address registered in your account.
  2. In the subject line, write: “Account Deletion Request – BettaBase Management”.
  3. In the email, include:
    • Your account email address
    • Your full name (optional)
    • Confirmation that you want all your account data deleted

What data will be deleted

We will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account email and authentication information
  • App usage data linked to your account
  • Pro purchase status and subscription records (if stored)

What data may be retained

Some data may be retained for legal or administrative reasons, including:

  • Payment confirmation records from Google Play (as required by Google/financial regulations)
  • Logs required for fraud prevention or legal compliance

Deletion timeframe

After receiving your request, we will process the deletion within 30 days.

If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact us again at bettafishapk@gmail.com.
